Few Words About Me

Hello, I'm Glavincheski Sreten, a Computer Science student, currently studying at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering - Skopje, with a passion for artificial intelligence and Machine Learning. I find this ever-evolving field of artificial intelligence fascinating, and I'm enthusiastic about exploring its applications and advancements. In my free time i like to explore the world of Web Development and play around wide variety of projects, further expanding my knowledge of how to internet works.


Growing up in Struga, where i got my formal education, i've always been interested in learning about new technologies ever since i got my first personal computer. Throughout the years this interest has become a passion and that's why i was super excited to start my journey this year in the IT world.


Even without previous job experience, I am constantly engaged in working on self-made projects that contribute to my expanding knowledge of the tech world and computer science. With a keen interest in web development, I invest my time and effort into honing my skills and exploring new concepts within this field.


This is a short personal webpage (portfolio site) where i take the chance of introducing myself, taking about who i am, what are my interests and hobbies, and what future projects i'm planning.

My Passion

Here are some of my hobbies and passions!

Chess


Chess has been one of my biggest interest throughout my life, from when I was a child to the present. I've been fascinated by chess's complexities and the tactical difficulties it poses ever since I first held one in my hands. My passion for chess only deepened as the years passed. The game has become a constant friend, giving me insightful lessons in patience, foresight, and critical thinking. Great joy of mine is witnessing the brilliance of world-class chess players as they compete on the global stage. A dream of mine is to meet the great people that helped popularize the game of chess and have invented many strategies making it more fun and enjoyable.

Swimming


Swimming has been an integral part of my life, stemming from my roots in the beautiful city of Struga, known for its natural wonders and home to one of Europe's oldest lakes, Ohrid Lake. My earliest memories revolve around the beach, where I would eagerly splash and play, creating unforgettable moments of pure joy. Whenever an opportunity arises, I find myself drawn to the inviting waters, craving the sensation of a refreshing swim. Although I do not partake in competitive swimming, I find immense pleasure in indulging in a leisurely swim. It is during these moments that I find true joy and passion to this wonderful sport.
